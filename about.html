THE LOGO PATROL

A PLACE WHERE PEOPLE GROW PEOPLE

For years, Businesses had their own footing on building great things. Over the years, people built very many great things, made structures so high, built cities so vast. Some people, businesses, brands were remembered. Some, alas were forgotten. It's apparent that in this day and age, you need proper branding to establish yourselves in the competitive environment that surrounds us. With that fact in mind, The Logo Patrol was born in 2014.

We started small, targeting the small Facebook communities. We relied on Word-Of-Mouth. We believe, as a venture, this is where everything went right for us. Over the years we have made great connections, networked with so many great brands. And we have helped build so many amazing brands around Sri Lanka. And we're damn proud of that.

WHAT DO WE DO? WE BUILD GREAT BRANDS

With the help of an awesome team, we build great brands. It maybe a Logo, an Emblem, Stationary Works or Social Media, we have helped build a lot of amazing brands that have spread their wings in so many great areas. Everyday, we build more. Enrich more. Be, more.
